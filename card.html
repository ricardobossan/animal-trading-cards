Syberian Husky

Huskies have a thick three layered coat, for extremely cold enviroments.

  • Scientific Name:: Canis Familiaris
  • Average Lenght:: 51 - 60 cm
  • Average Lifespan:: 12 - 15 years
  • Habitat:: cold enviroments

The Husky loves cold enviroments and is extremely curious; easily bored; very playful; enjoys company, even tough may not seem like it sometimes.
